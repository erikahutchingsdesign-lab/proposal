Summary

A two-track transformation

One platform, two ways it creates value — first by transforming how our own team works, then by turning those same tools into a subscription product for clients.

01
Track One · Internal
Transform how we work
Stop the team's subscriptions to traditional, pricey software and platforms.
Shift focus to expanding our proprietary apps and tools — and using them as our work engine.
A very lean, mean team — fewer hours, but far more output.
No outsourcing needed.
02
Track Two · Client
Give clients the tools
Give clients access to these same apps in a custom, branded version.
They generate their own work using our apps on the "Powered by Havener" platform — while under contract with Havener.
Subscription-based, with ongoing upgrades, new templates, and features to keep them engaged and wanting more.
How the team supports it
Head of Content Build the backend voice and make sure it's accurate.
Head of Creative Evolve the platform and customize output for every new client — plus ongoing upgrades for current ones.
Key Takeaways — Suggested New Role for Me
01
Chief Creative & AI Officer
Or a similar title — a new role, with compensation to match, leading the build, rollout, and ongoing evolution of the platform across both the internal team and the client product.

Growth-linked compensation
Compensation tracked to company performance and overall growth — aligned with the results the platform drives.
03
Commission on subscriptions
Upside through commission on the recurring client subscription revenue the platform generates as it scales.
